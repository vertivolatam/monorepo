## Revenue Streams

4 revenue streams in order of contribution:

1. **SaaS Monitoring** — Largest stream. Tiered plans: Basic, Pro, Commercial, Industrial
2. **Marketplace de Insumos** — Caja Vertivo subscription + on-demand purchases
3. **Hardware Sales** — Vertivo Home, Pro, Scale (acquisition vehicle, not growth engine)
4. **Data & B2B** — Enjambre Data API, Agronomic Insights Reports, White-label licensing
